## Community Support

The [Terragrunt Community Discord Server](/tgs-discord) is the best place to ask questions, share tips, and get help from other Terragrunt Scale users and Gruntwork engineers.

<Aside type="tip" title="Enterprise Support">

[Terragrunt Enterprise Support](https://www.gruntwork.io/services/terragrunt) is available for organizations that need dedicated support, SLAs, and direct access to the Gruntwork engineering team.

</Aside>
